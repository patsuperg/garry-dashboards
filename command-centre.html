Methodology▾

Net A$/mo is each door's stored owner net, converted at the live USD→AUD rate.

After reserves holds back 8.33% of gross rent for vacancy plus a flat $100/month for CapEx, per door — a more conservative number than the raw stored net.

HAP share is the percentage of a door's rent paid by the housing voucher rather than the tenant — green ≥90%, amber 60–90%, red <60%. Higher is less tenant-dependent income.

This month compares each door's actually-observed receipt this month against its expected rent.

The Moves are ranked purely by $/mo impact; a large one-off dollar amount (like a tax delinquency) can still land in "+N more" if its $/mo impact is zero.

Month-over-month trend is intentionally not shown on the scoreboard: the only historical ledger available is mid-month for the current period and measures actual dollars collected — a different basis than this scoreboard's stored owner-net figure. Showing a trend arrow here would compare two different things, so it stays off until a same-basis history exists.

New deals found automatically — every day at 9am

✅
Patrick decides — everything else is pre-solved
Patrick reviews the deal summary and repair estimate. If it passes, he sends the offer. All context (comps, cash flow model, crime data, rent demand) is already attached. The only decision is yes or no.

Repair quotes drafted and sent to contractors automatically
The moment a property goes under contract, the system drafts professional repair scope emails to vetted contractors and saves them as Gmail Drafts. Patrick reviews and sends with one tap. Bids are logged and compared automatically.
🏘️
Property passes Section 8 inspection — tenant placed by PM
Property manager (Avenue/Tactical) handles tenant screening, leasing, and Section 8 voucher approval. Once approved, HUD pays rent directly to the PM who disburses to Patrick monthly.
💰
Rental income tracked and reconciled automatically
The system reads Avenue distribution emails, scrapes the property management portals, and cross-checks against expected amounts per property. If payment is missing on day 8 of the month, a polite chase email is drafted automatically. Day 12 with no response = escalation email to Patrick.
📊
Monthly P&L generated — no accounting software needed
Every Monday, a clean income vs expense report is generated per property and saved as a spreadsheet. Tax shield monitors assessment spikes and drafts appeal letters if needed. Every 6 months, competitive insurance quotes are requested automatically to keep premiums low.
