Our Story

The life we chose to build together

This isn't a card. It's a record — of every moment that brought us here, and every reason I would choose you again without thinking twice.

August 2019 · Melbourne
Saturday night. No plan. No idea.

We met on a Saturday night in August 2019. Neither of us saw it coming. What started as one night turned into one of those rare, suspended stretches of time where the rest of the world just... stops. We stayed up. We spent the whole Sunday together. Somewhere around the 30-hour mark it stopped feeling like a date and started feeling like something else entirely.

Monday, 26 August 2019 · 6:45am
I left. But I didn't want to.

The Monday morning after we met, I was on a 6:45am flight to Sydney to buy a business — fit out a store, hire staff, build something from scratch for Spartans. I was gone 3–4 weeks, back and forth interstate, barely surfacing. We went from a 30-hour date to me essentially disappearing interstate the next morning. I've always thought that was the most chaotic possible start to a relationship. And somehow it held.

The Beginning
Two people. One yes.

Before any of the big moments, there was a choice — to lean in, to stay, to keep showing up. That quiet, daily decision turned into everything.

January 2025 · Malaysia
Chinese New Year, Family, and Your Loud Laugh

KL with the family. My dad, his partner, the chaos of a table full of people who love you. You fit right in — you always do. You made it feel like home even when we were 7,000 km from ours.

7 February 2025 · Phuket, Thailand
Phuket Marriott. 40 people. The sea. You.

We stood in front of the people we love and chose each other. Not the perfect version. Not the polished version. The real, messy, beautiful version. And I'd do it every single day.

2025 — 2026
You built something real

While I was busy building a property portfolio, you were quietly building something far more important — a business rooted in helping people actually connect. Holding Space. I watched you go from an idea to a curriculum to a community. I've never been more proud of you.

April 2026 · Bangkok
A new city. The same us.

Bangkok. A fresh chapter with you in a place that doesn't know us yet. We get to build that story from scratch. I can't think of anyone else I'd want next to me for it.
