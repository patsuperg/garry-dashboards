What changed (the level-up)

🔴 Before

  • Talking to Garry meant opening iTerm, typing a special command, leaving the window minimised. If iTerm closed, Garry was offline.
  • Telegram noise — multiple status pings a day from various agents. "Audit complete," "Score 64/100," "All clear" — pages of stuff you didn't need to see.
  • Dropped balls — commitments made in one session weren't logged anywhere. Next session might forget. You had to remember.
  • Health data — you had a spoon score idea but the pipeline kept reading the wrong field, sending null numbers. No tempo for the day.
  • Cashflow — you'd export Wise statements manually to know where you stood.
  • Dictation — Apple's native missed every proper noun (Hazelcrest, Brannon, Lindia kept getting mangled).
  • Property pipeline — Garry had been making offers on your behalf without you seeing the property first. Money decisions slipping through.

🟢 Now

  • Chich daemon on Mini, headless. Message @chich_direct_bot from anywhere — voice or text — and you've got a full session with all the tools. Auto-restarts on crash. No iTerm needed, ever.
  • Garry daemon same pattern: @Garry_Task_Bot. Old clunky path retired.
  • Gem (your AI therapist, Meg-backwards) — terminal alias gem or Telegram /gem command. Loaded with all your inner-work context.
  • Cadence locked — Mon-Fri 8am brief, essential-only pings 8-5, silence after hours. 3+ chronic noise senders retired forever, Roger neutered to log-only.
  • Command Center — single URL showing every task across every agent. command-center.html. "Did anyone do X?" becomes structurally obsolete.
  • Health pipeline live — spoon score 0-12 with tempo frame. Auto-refresh every 30 min.
  • Wise cashflow daily-pull — \$113K balance, \$20K net last 30 days, per-PM reconciliation, missing-rent alerts. No manual exports.
  • Handy + custom dictionary across Air, Mini, iPhone — your terms (Hazelcrest, Brannon, Lindia, Empire Investing) all land first time.
  • Hard rule: No AI initiates a property offer without your eyes on it first. Iron-rule four protected forever.
  • Ohio watchpoint watcher — fires daily. Already flagged May 1 (Tactical payee change, 2 days out).
